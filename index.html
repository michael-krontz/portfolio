My Approach

Twenty years of doing the work. The last few learning how to lead it.

01

ALIGN + DISCOVER

Start by getting everyone in the same room. Good design strategy begins with understanding what people actually need and what the business is trying to accomplish. I don't skip this step.

02

DEFINE + STRATEGIZE

Turn research into direction. Clear frameworks, design principles, and a roadmap teams can actually execute. Alignment across design, product, and engineering before AI writes a line of code.

03

BUILD + LEAD

Guide the team through execution while keeping craft standards high. Coaching designers, protecting the design system, removing blockers, and making sure the work ships well. Not just ships.

04

MEASURE + GROW

Track outcomes, not just output. Use data and user feedback to improve the product and develop the team. The work is never really done, and neither is the growth.
