My Process

DISCOVERY + RESEARCH

Deep diving into user behavior, competitive analysis, and stakeholder interviews to define clear product opportunities and user needs.

DEFINE + IDEATE

Transforming research insights into user flows and wireframes, exploring solutions through rapid ideation and technical feasibility assessment.

PROTOTYPE + BUILD

Creating high-fidelity prototypes and building native mobile applications, ensuring seamless user experiences across platforms.

LAUNCH + ITERATE

Taking products from design to app store launch, implementing analytics, and continuously improving based on user data and feedback.
